Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I make a booking?

Browse the Hotels or Events page and click the “Book Now” button. Then fill out the form and submit your booking.

2. Can I cancel or edit my booking?

Yes. Visit the User Dashboard to view, modify, or cancel your bookings.

3. How do I contact customer support?

Go to the Contact Us page and fill out the message form. You can also send an email directly to the Leaders Team.

4. What information do I need to make a booking?

You will need your name, email address, and booking details such as check-in date, event date, or number of guests.

5. Is my personal information secure?

Yes. SmartBooking uses safe form submission and secure hosting to keep your information private.

6. Which payments are supported?

This demo project does not include payment integration. In real systems, credit/debit cards and secure gateways are used.
